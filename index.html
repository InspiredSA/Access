How to register

Complete the registration in six steps

Open the registration link

02

03

04

Upload a photograph

05

Review and submit

Check the information before submitting to avoid delays in processing.

06

Wait for confirmation

Your school will confirm once the registration has been processed.

Questions

Frequently asked questions

Who needs to register?

Parents, guardians, or approved adults who need access for arrival, collection, or school visits may be asked to register. Please follow the guidance sent by your school.

What type of photograph should I upload?

Use a recent, clear photograph of your face. Avoid sunglasses, heavy shadows, filters, group photographs, or images where your face is partly covered.

Can both parents or guardians register?

In most cases, each parent or guardian who requires access should complete their own registration. Your school will confirm any local requirements.

What should I do if I make a mistake?

Please contact your school office as soon as possible. They will advise whether the information can be corrected or whether a new submission is required.

Who do I contact for help?

If you are unsure about any part of the registration process, please contact your school office.
